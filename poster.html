Everyone has the right to life, liberty and security of person. And also to protect and preserve every individual's humanity, to ensure that every individual can live a life of dignity and a life that is worthy of a human being. Human rights are basic rights and freedoms that belong to every person, regardless of nationality, race, gender, religion, or any other status. These rights are based on the principles of dignity, equality, and respect, and they are meant to ensure that everyone can live with freedom and justice. Human rights encompass a wide range of protections, including the right to life, liberty, and personal security, freedom of speech and religion, the right to work and receive an education and protection against discrimination.
